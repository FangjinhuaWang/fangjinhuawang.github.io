Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at ETH Zurich, supervised by Prof. Marc Pollefeys. My research interests lie in the field of 3D Vision and machine learning. Previously, I obtained a Master’s degree in Robotics at ETH zurich. I also spent some time working on multi-view stereo at Microsoft Mixed Reality & AI Lab Zurich.
